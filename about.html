About Ragnarok Online

Ragnarok Online is a massively multiplayer online role-playing game (MMORPG) developed and published by the South Korean company Gravity Co., Ltd. The game was first released in 2002 and quickly gained popularity due to its anime-inspired art style, engaging gameplay mechanics, and deep customization options. Set in the fictional world of Rune Midgard, players take on the roles of adventurers, exploring towns, dungeons, and battlefields while participating in various quests, combat, and social activities.
Milestones Details
2002: Ragnarok Online Launches in South Korea The game is officially launched in South Korea, marking the beginning of its international success.
2003-2005: International Expansion Ragnarok Online expands internationally, reaching multiple regions including North America, Japan, and Southeast Asia.
2006-2009: Introduction of Expansions & Growth Multiple expansions are released, and the community continues to grow with new content and features.
2010-2014: Launch of Ragnarok Online 2 and Mobile Versions The game launches a 3D version (Ragnarok Online 2) and a mobile adaptation, though the original remains more popular.
2015-2020: Mobile Versions Gain Popularity Mobile versions like Ragnarok M: Eternal Love become major hits, bringing new players into the franchise.
2021-present: Nostalgia & Continued Success The original Ragnarok Online sees a nostalgic resurgence with private servers, while Ragnarok M: Eternal Love continues to thrive with regular updates.
